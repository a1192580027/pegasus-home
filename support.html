Support & Care

Basic guidance for caring for your Pegasus unit.
• Keep Pegasus in a dry indoor environment.
• For surface cleaning, use the included soft care cloth.
• Avoid strong detergents or bleaching agents.
• Do not expose to open flames or direct high heat sources.
• For long-term storage, keep Pegasus in its original box or a dust-free space.
