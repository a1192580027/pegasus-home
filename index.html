SAFETY & PRIVACY

Cute on the outside, carefully controlled inside.

A robot that lives in your house needs clear rules. Pegasus makes it easy to see what he is doing and easy to say “stop” at any time.

You stay in control.

  • One-tap privacy modes with clear lights when microphones are active.
  • Local processing for common tasks; sensitive data is encrypted end-to-end.
  • Easy-to-read activity log so parents and guardians can review what happened.
Safety is not about hiding technology – it is about making it understandable to everyone in the house.
Emotion layer limits

Pegasus does not simulate anger, insults, or sarcasm. His range is carefully kept within calm, supportive, and lightly playful responses.

In other words: the worst thing he can do is remind you kindly, one more time.
